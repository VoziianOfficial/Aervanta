Aervanta Privacy Policy

Aervanta is an independent HVAC provider-matching platform. This policy describes how information may be handled when you visit the website, submit a request, contact us, or interact with platform features.

1. Information we collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, including your name, email address, phone number, selected HVAC service category, message details, source page, and privacy consent status.

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate interaction timing, cookie consent choice, pages visited, and form usability signals. This information helps maintain website functionality and request processing.

2. How information is used

Request information may be used to receive and organize your HVAC-related request, identify a relevant service path, respond to your inquiry, improve website usability, and support independent provider-matching where available.

Aervanta does not use request information to diagnose HVAC equipment, approve providers, guarantee pricing, guarantee scheduling, certify provider credentials, or create a service agreement.

3. Provider matching and information sharing

When you submit a request, your information may be shared with participating independent providers or matching partners for the purpose of helping you compare available local provider options.

Participating providers are independent from Aervanta. Final pricing, availability, scheduling, warranties, licenses, insurance, service terms, and outcomes are provided by those providers and should be reviewed directly by the homeowner.

4. Cookies and localStorage

Aervanta may use essential cookies or localStorage to remember cookie consent choices, improve navigation, support form usability, and maintain basic website functionality. The cookie consent banner remains visible until you accept or decline.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Declining non-essential storage may limit some convenience features, but core page navigation should remain available.

5. Your choices

You may choose not to submit a request. You may contact Aervanta to ask about request information you previously submitted, request correction, or ask that we stop contacting you where legally and operationally possible.

If an independent provider contacts you after a request, you should communicate directly with that provider about their own privacy practices, terms, scheduling, quote, and service agreement.

6. Security and retention

Aervanta uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate for a request-matching website. No website or email transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

Request information may be retained as needed to process inquiries, support platform operations, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records.

7. Children’s privacy

Aervanta is intended for homeowners and adults seeking HVAC provider options. The site is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
