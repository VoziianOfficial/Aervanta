Aervanta Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies, localStorage, and similar browser storage may be used on the Aervanta website. Aervanta is an independent HVAC provider-matching platform and does not perform HVAC work directly.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files or storage entries placed on your browser or device by a website. They can help a website remember choices, support basic functionality, improve user experience, and understand how visitors interact with pages.

Similar technologies, including localStorage and sessionStorage, may store information in your browser without creating a traditional cookie file.

2. How Aervanta may use browser storage

Aervanta may use browser storage to remember cookie banner choices, maintain basic website preferences, support navigation behavior, improve form usability, and help the website operate consistently across pages.

Browser storage is not used by Aervanta to diagnose HVAC systems, approve providers, guarantee quotes, guarantee availability, or create any service agreement.

3. Essential cookies and storage

Essential storage may be necessary for core website functionality. For example, Aervanta may store whether you accepted or declined the cookie banner so the banner does not need to appear repeatedly after a choice is made.

Essential storage may also support accessibility, navigation, form timing checks, spam prevention, and basic page functionality.

4. Analytics and performance technologies

Aervanta may use analytics or performance tools to understand general website usage, page performance, and visitor interaction patterns. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies depending on the setup used by the website owner.

Analytics information should be interpreted as website usage data only. It does not verify provider credentials, provider quality, pricing, licensing, insurance, or HVAC service outcomes.

5. Request-form related storage

The contact/request form may use technical fields or browser-side timing values to reduce spam and improve request processing. This helps protect the form from automated submissions and supports basic request handling.

Submitting a request does not guarantee provider contact, quote availability, emergency response, scheduling, or service completion.

6. Your cookie choices

You may accept or decline cookies through the website banner where available. You may also control cookies and browser storage through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or restrict cookies. If you block all cookies or storage, some convenience features may not work as expected, though core page content should remain accessible.

7. Third-party tools

Some website features may rely on third-party libraries, hosting tools, analytics services, font services, map tools, security features, or embedded resources. These third parties may use their own cookies or storage technologies according to their own policies.

Aervanta is not responsible for third-party cookie practices. You should review the privacy and cookie policies of any third-party service you interact with.

8. Updates to this Cookie Policy

Aervanta may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in website functionality, legal requirements, or platform operations. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
